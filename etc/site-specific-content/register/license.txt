<p>
In order, to release your project, <b>you should write copyright notices and 
license notices at the beginning of every source code file</b>, and include a 
copy of the plain text version of the license. If your software is published 
under the GNU GPL license, you should read 
<a href="http://www.gnu.org/copyleft/gpl.html#SEC4" target="_blank">the section
 about applying license terms to your program</a>.
</p>

<p>
For many legal reasons, 
you may not change a project's license once it has been set. If you
feel that you have a special case and legal capability to do this,
we will work with you on a case-by-case basis.
</p>
