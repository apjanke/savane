Registration process

User registration is a simple, automatic process. It only requires the introduction of user name, password, e-mail address, and real name. A confirmation mail is automatically sent to the given address, with an URL that must be visited to activate the registration.

It is very important to provide a valid, frequently visited email address, since in addition to registration activation, it will be used to send notifications of all events that might affect, or be of interest to the user.
